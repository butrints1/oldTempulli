Evaluated results of learning in schooling "Driving Instructor"

Evaluated results of learning in schooling "Driving Instructor"

  • News
  • Author tempulli
  • Date & Time:
    • January 18, 2020

Prishtina, 18 January 2020

After several months of training by the group of trainees for the Qualification of "Driving Instructor", the acquired knowledge of the candidates in each module in the theoretical and practical part of education has started to be valued.

This qualification is already a new standard in the profession, accredited by the National Qualifications Authority and authorized by the Ministry of Infrastructure and Transport.

Tempulli College has been providing this training since 2006 and has continuously provided other trainings in this field through the Professional Development Center (PDC).
